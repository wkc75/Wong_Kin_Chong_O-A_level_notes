# Atoms and Subatomic Particles

An atom is the smallest component of an element having the chemical properties of the element.

All atoms are made of up the same three subatomic particles

- eletrons
- protons
- neutrons

The protons and neutrons make up the nucleus of the atom. Thus they are also referred to as nucleons. The electrons are surrounding the nucleus.

# **Electronegativity**

<!--prettier-ignore-->
!!! definition "Definition"
    **Electronegativity** of an atom is a measure of its **ability to attract the electrons** in a **covalent bond** to itself.

## **Factors affecting electronegativity**

### **Across the Period**

- Number of quantum shells remains constant.
- **Nuclear charge increases** while **shielding effect remains relatively constant**.
- **Effective Nuclear Charge increases** across the period.
- Thus **electronegativity increases**.

### **Down the Group**

- **Nuclear charge and shielding effect both increases**.
- **Effective nulear charge remains relatively constant**
- **Number of quantum shell increases**
- Thus, **electronegativity decreases**

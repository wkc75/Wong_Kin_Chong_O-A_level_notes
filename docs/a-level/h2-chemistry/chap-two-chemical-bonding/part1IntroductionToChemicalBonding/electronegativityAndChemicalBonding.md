# **ELectronegativity and Chemical Bonding**

The **difference in electronegativity** between two atoms can be used to **predict the type of bond** between them.

$$
\text{similar electronegativity}
\;\longrightarrow\;
\text{electron sharing}
\;\longrightarrow\;
\text{covalent bonding}
$$

$$
\text{large difference in electronegativity}
\;\longrightarrow\;
\text{electron transfer}
\;\longrightarrow\;
\text{ionic bonding}
$$

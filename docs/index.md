# **Wong Kin Chong O/A level Notes**

Welcome to my notes website. I am Kin Chong, currently studying Computer Science in NUS.
On the side, I give tuition for O level and A level students on math and sciences.

## **What's this website for**

This website is created to support my students in their revision and independent learning. It provides an alternative set of notes for those who may find school materials insufficient or difficult to follow.

At the same time, I believe that education should be accessible to everyone. Not all students can afford tuition, so this platform is open to anyone who wishes to learn, revise, and improve at their own pace.

In the future, I hope to expand this website to include more features, such as a discussion forum, progress tracking, quizzes, and interactive visualisations. However, with current academic commitments, development may take some time. Thank you for your patience and support.
